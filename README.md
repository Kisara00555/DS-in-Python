A Python-based AI system using LLM integration to solve real-world problems through modular components and intelligent decision-making.
# AI Startup Funding & Investment Intelligence Assistant

## Problem Statement

Startup founders and entrepreneurs often need to search through multiple funding guides, venture capital playbooks, startup investment documents, and fundraising resources to understand key concepts such as SAFE agreements, startup valuation, dilution, seed funding, and investor expectations.

Traditional LLMs may generate inaccurate information or lack access to specialized startup funding documents. This project uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to retrieve information from a private corpus of startup funding and investment documents and generate grounded responses.

## Knowledge Scope

The system focuses on:

* Startup Funding Fundamentals
* Investment Agreements
* Startup Valuation
* Investor Expectations
* Fundraising Process

The system does not cover:

* Stock Market Investing
* Cryptocurrency Investing
* Personal Finance
* Real Estate Investing
* Tax Advice
